Biography

My favorite things are motorcycles, longboards, heavy metal, turtles, Siamese cats, pizza, and apple pie. I'm a gamer and play lots of first person shooters and Starcraft II. I'm a software engineer and website developer. (It's why most of my projects are web-based!) Ubuntu is my primary operating system, have an Ubuntu phone and spend way too much time yelling at Nvidia's terrible Linux drivers. I'm an avid book reader, my favorite author is Brandon Sanderson, a god of Fantasy and Science Fiction. I also really enjoy the ponderings of many Science Fiction authors such as Isaac Asimov and Arthur C. Clarke.
