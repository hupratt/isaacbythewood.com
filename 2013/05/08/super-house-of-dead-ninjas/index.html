Super House of Dead Ninjas

08 May 2013 - Gaming

An incredible fast paced platformer. You have to fight your way down to the bottom of a tower killing monsters, collecting items, and getting upgrades. The tower is randomly generated and you get new items everytime. I enjoyed playing this game for quite a few hours and could easily get in the groove and zone out. A perfect game after a long day's work for me.

While I can't highly recommend this game I still think it's a fun time waster and worth a few playthroughs, it's on steam.
