Natural Selection 2

18 Oct 2012 - Gaming

If you enjoy RTS or FPS this is a great game, if you enjoy both it's the greatest game. I really enjoy playing an alien commander and building upgrades and bases tossing out cysts everywhere, or playing the extremely team oriented marines and helping clear out a hallway of skulks. Lots of fun no matter how you play it.

Only warning I have is that there is a steep learning curve, don't play commander for a while and do play the tutorial, you really need to learn how to play it or your team will hate you.

I highly recommend checking it out immediately, it's on steam!
